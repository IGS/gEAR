Instructions

  • Drag to re-arrange the dataset displays in any order you wish.
  • Since this is a grid system, tiles can be freely dragged anywhere on the grid.
  • You can also drag the right or bottom edges or corner of any display to change its width or height.
  • Dragging or resizing below the bottom row will create a new row to work with.
  • Tiles have the potential to overlap, and saving is disabled until all overlaps are resolved.
  • Click "Save" to save both single- and multigene arrangements.
