Create Account

Creating a gEAR account allows you to access all that gEAR has to offer. This includes:

  • Upload datasets - Upload your data for use on the gEAR.
  • Dataset privacy - You control whether your data is publically available to others or kept private for only you (or your team) to use.
  • Share datasets - Share data with collaborators and your team.
  • Customized workspace - Rearrange and reorder datasets that best suits you.

Step 1: Click on "Create Account"

To create a gEAR account, simply CLICK "Create Account."


Step 2: Complete the create account form

Then complete the following form and CLICK "Create Account." If you would like to receive news, updates, and new features about gEAR sure to check "Get regular e-mail updates."
