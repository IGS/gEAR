Note-taking Introduction

gEAR allows you to take, and save, notes during your gene expression search and analysis. The notes are specfic to a dataset, so each dataset has it's own set of notes. The following sections give details on these aspects of note-taking:

  1. Accessing Notes
  2. Notes Panel Overview
  3. Creating & Editing Notes
  4. Note Privacy (Private vs Public)

Accessing notes

To access gEAR Notes, you must be logged on with your gEAR account. Notes can be accessed on the main gEAR page and the dataset manager.
