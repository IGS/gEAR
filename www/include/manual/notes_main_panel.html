Notes Main Panel

On the right is the main panel for Notes. It lists all of the notes on a dataset; these include:

  • Your private notes
  • All public notes

The notes panel can be docked on left or right side of the screen, or be popped out into a separate window. The pop-out view allows for a larger working space. These options are accessed with the button.

Only notes for the selected dataset will appear. You can write as many notes as you want.

For each note, you can quickly view the:

  • Title
  • Author
  • Time of last updated
  • Access level - Public notes are labelled
  • Content preview

To help find a particular note, notes can be sorted by date or title and public notes can be hidden, or displayed.
