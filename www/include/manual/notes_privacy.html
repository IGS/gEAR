Note Privacy

We understand your privacy is important. Therefore, we make it possible for your notes to be private - "for your eyes only". If you so choose, you can later make the note public for others to see.


Keep in mind that public notes are visible to those can has access to the dataset. If you post a public note on a public dataset or a dataset that is shared with others, other gEAR users will see it.
