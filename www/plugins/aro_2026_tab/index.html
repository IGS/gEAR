  • ARO 2026 Workshop Sessions

    Join us at the ARO 2026 49th Annual MidWinter Meeting for two interactive gEAR workshops designed to enhance your research capabilities.

    Beginner workshop - Introduction to gEAR

    Date: Friday, February 6, 2026
    Time: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
    Room: Meeting Room 208ABC, Floor 2

    This introductory workshop provides a hands-on tour of the Gene Expression Analysis Resource (gEAR). Participants will learn how to create an account, navigate the user dashboard, and search across gEAR’s growing library of datasets. We’ll cover the basics of finding and viewing gene expression profiles, organizing results into lists, and exploring curated dataset collections.

    Workshop 2: Advanced workshop - User stories

    Date: Sunday, February 8, 2026
    Time: 4:15 PM - 5:15 PM
    Room: Meeting Room 209ABC, Floor 2

    Join us for an exciting workshop where you’ll learn directly from leading investigators how to harness the power of the gEAR platform to explore cutting-edge, newly generated multi-omic datasets in our field—and accelerate your own research. We’ll kick off with a brief introduction from the gEAR team, followed by three, brief, expert-led sessions.
