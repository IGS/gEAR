  • Hearing Restoration Project

    The Hearing Restoration Project (HRP) is a collaborative effort to accelerate research to promote hair cell regeneration. The consortium comprises thirteen laboratories in the US and Canada that work to identify roadblocks in the field, develop resources, and share data.

    Current efforts focus on defining the molecular signatures of mature hair cells and identifying cellular signals that enable supporting cells to regenerate functional hair cells. The goal is to develop approaches to drive hair cell regeneration in the human cochlea, to heal hearing loss and tinnitus.
