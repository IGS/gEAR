Workshops at ARO 2023

Workshop schedule

Saturday 4:15pm - 5:30

Introduction

This will be a walk-through of the basic features of the portal, giving the background needed for the more tool-specific sessions. Topics include account creation, basic gene expression searches, data sharing, site navigation and overall feature discussion. We will also overview some of the new features of gEAR and point users to online webinars where they can learn more about them.

Visit our helpdesk at the meeting for personalized assistance or to learn more about gEAR!

Location: Helpdesk and all sessions are located in Biscayne 1
